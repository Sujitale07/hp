Ascending Tranquility: Trekking the Annapurna Base Camp

The Annapurna Base Camp Trek is a mesmerizing journey through the breathtaking landscapes of the Annapurna region in Nepal. This iconic trek takes adventurers deep into the heart of the Annapurna Sanctuary, surrounded by towering peaks, lush forests, and picturesque villages.

Itinerary

An itinerary is a detailed plan or schedule outlining activities, events, and travel arrangements for a specific period, such as a vacation, business trip, or event

Day

1

Kathmandu to Pokhara

During the first day of the retreat, participants will gather at the designated location in the morning for an introductory session, where they will receive a warm welcome and undergo an orientation to familiarize themselves with the agenda. Following this, engaging ice-breaking activities and team-building exercises are scheduled to encourage camaraderie among attendees.

Afternoon arrangements include a hearty lunch buffet followed by a group discussion to outline the company's goals for the retreat. Participants will then have free time to unwind and explore the resort facilities at their leisure. In the evening, a group dinner at the resort restaurant will provide an opportunity for informal networking and socializing.

Day

2

Pokhara to Nayapul, and trek to Tikhedhunga

On the second day, the focus shifts to team-building initiatives and workshops aimed at enhancing leadership skills and fostering effective communication among team members. The morning begins with a delicious breakfast followed by a series of professionally-led team-building activities. Mid-morning, there will be a brief coffee break to recharge before delving into workshop sessions dedicated to leadership development and communication strategies. Lunch will be served buffet-style, allowing participants to refuel for the afternoon's outdoor adventure activities, such as hiking or zip-lining. The day concludes with a memorable BBQ dinner enjoyed under the stars by the campfire.

Day

3

Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani

Trek from Tikhedhunga to Ghorepani (approximately 6-7 hours). Ghorepani is famous for its panoramic views of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges.

Day

4

Ghorepani to Tadapani via Poon Hill

Early morning hike to Poon Hill for sunrise views. Trek from Ghorepani to Tadapani (approximately 5-6 hours).
